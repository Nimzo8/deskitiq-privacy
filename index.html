DeskitIQ Privacy Policy

DeskitIQ is designed to clean your Windows desktop safely. Preview every move before anything changes.

Effective date: April 18, 2026

Who We Are

DeskitIQ is published by Nimzo. This policy explains how DeskitIQ handles information when you use the Windows desktop app.

Contact: deskitiq.support@gmail.com

What DeskitIQ Does

DeskitIQ scans the top-level items on your Windows desktop, builds a cleanup plan, and lets you preview proposed moves before organizing. The app is intended to help you review, organize, back up, and restore desktop cleanup changes safely.

Information We Collect

DeskitIQ primarily works locally on your device. The app may read information about desktop items, such as file names, folder names, shortcut information, file types, file sizes, and modified dates, so it can build a cleanup preview and organize selected items.

DeskitIQ does not upload your desktop files to Nimzo servers.

The current version is not designed to send analytics, telemetry, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or automatic update-check data to Nimzo. If those features are added in a future version, this policy should be updated before that version is released.

Local Processing and Local Storage

Desktop analysis happens locally on your Windows device. Cleanup plans, settings, ignored items, remembered choices, and app history may be stored locally so DeskitIQ can provide undo, restore, and review features.

You can remove local app data by using DeskitIQ settings where available, uninstalling the app, or manually deleting local app data created by DeskitIQ.

Backups, Manifests, and Restore History

Before organizing selected items, DeskitIQ can create local backup manifests and cleanup history records. These records may include original file paths, destination paths, item names, categories, timestamps, and cleanup results.

This information is used to help you undo or restore cleanup changes. Backup manifests and restore history are stored locally on your device unless you choose to copy, export, sync, or share them yourself.

Data Sharing

Nimzo does not sell your personal information. DeskitIQ does not upload your desktop files to Nimzo servers, and Nimzo does not receive your desktop contents through the normal use of the app.

If you contact Nimzo for support, you may choose to share information such as screenshots, logs, receipts, or descriptions of your issue. We use support information only to respond to your request and improve the app.

Third-Party Services

The current version of DeskitIQ is not designed to use third-party analytics, advertising, telemetry, or cloud file-processing services.

If you install DeskitIQ through the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may process information related to store installation, licensing, purchases, updates, crash reporting, and device compatibility under Microsoft’s own privacy terms.

Children’s Privacy

DeskitIQ is a general Windows utility and is not directed to children. Nimzo does not knowingly collect personal information from children through DeskitIQ.

Changes to This Policy

Nimzo may update this privacy policy when DeskitIQ changes or when legal, store, or operational requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when the policy changes.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or DeskitIQ privacy practices, contact Nimzo at deskitiq.support@gmail.com.
